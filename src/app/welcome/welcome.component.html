What is FF7:SES?

Final Fantasy 7: Shinra Employment Services is a challenge run for the original FF7, similar in vein to what you might see in FF5's "Four Job Fiesta" challenge runs. The basic premise is rather than being able to use any Materia on any character you please, additional challenge is placed on the run via forcing each playable character into pre-generated (using this very website) jobs. For example, your Cloud may draw the job of the Pyromaniac and be limited to the following Materia: Fire, All, Elemental, Final Attack, MP Turbo, Magic Counter, Quadra Magic, Magic Plus, Mega All, MP Plus, Ifrit, and Phoenix.

Why do I need to log in?

Logging in allows the site to keep track of multiple runs for a single user. So by logging in, you are allowed to have more than one run registered on the site at a time. Your runs can have the chance of jobs being repeated, or be forced to have every job be unique via a switch on the New Run page.
